Check my Czech will be an online czech grammar learing tool aimed at self-learners or anyone taking a course that wants to practise their grammar. It will begin will noun declensions, and plans to include all facets of Czech grammar, including declining adjectives, pronouns and possesive pronouns. May also include conjugations, passives and anything requested by users (or possibly the ability for users to add there own examples). Their are eventual plans for social network log-in and integration.

Check my Czech will initially be free, but there may eventually be a small fee for the full features as they are added. By backing this bitstarter, for an extremely modest fee you will get an acknowledgement on the app's webpage as well as free access to any future versions of Check my Czech. You will also be supporting the development costs and letting me know there is a market for the app.

FAQ

Aren't there similar things already available online/on my iPhone?
Not as far as I'm aware: Check my Czech is only for grammar, not vocabulary, and is designed from the bottom up with Czech in mind.
